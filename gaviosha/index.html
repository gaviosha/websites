Hello! Welcome to Shakeel's academic website. I am a PhD student and Fellow in Statistics at the London School of Economics and Political Science; I am part of the Time Series and Statistical Learning research group, and am fortunate to be supervised by Professor Piotr Fryzlewicz. Some of my research interests include:
  • Multiscale statistical modeling
  • Change points and feature detection
  • Shape constrained estimation
  • Aspects of causal inference
